Initializes the rendering pipeline for the Depth Camera. Configures the Scene\+Capture\+Component to read raw Scene\+Depth instead of Post\+Process HDR. Disables translucency and atmospheric effects to ensure the camera rays ignore custom water assets and strictly measure solid geometry distances.
